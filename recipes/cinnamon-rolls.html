Cinnamon Rolls

Enjoy these cinnamon rolls created by my mother! Perfect for a cozy morning breakfast with some coffee.

Ingredients

Dough

  • ¾ cup milk
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 2 ½ tsp quick yeast
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups bread flour
  • 1 tsp cooking oil

Filling

  • 4 Tbsp powdered sugar
  • 3 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp softened butter

Frosting

Stir ingredients together and store in a fridge for up to 4 days.
  • ½ stick of cream cheese
  • ¾ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place milk, butter, and white sugar in a small pan. Heat on the stove at medium heat until the butter is halfway melted (Test the temperature by sticking a clean finger in for 5 seconds, should be warm and comfortable).
  2. Stir in the yeast. Add in the egg and stir. Pour this liquid into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add all-purpose flour and ½ bread flour, stir for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining bread flour gradually, stir to form a ball.
  5. Knead the dough for 4-5 minutes. Shape the dough into a ball by pulling and tucking in the sides.
  6. Drip 1 tsp of cooking oil into the bowl, coat the dough in the oil. Cover with a lid and let it rise in a warm place for 50-70 minutes, or until the dough has doubled in size.
  7. Mix the dry filling ingredients (sugars and cinnamon) together.
  8. Remove the dough from the bowl with a rubber spatula onto a large cutting board. Flatten the dough and roll into a 9x12 inch rectangle. The dough should be ⅜ inches thick.
  9. Spread the soft butter on the dough evenly. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture on top, make sure to save 1 Tbsp.
  10. Slice into 6 or 8 rolls, depending on how big you want the servings. Place the cut-side down on a parchment paper lined baking pan, space them 1 ½ to 2 inches apart. Sprinkle the remaining sugar mixture on top of each roll. Keep the rolls covered in plastic wrap or parchment paper, let it rise for about 50-60 minutes or until doubled in size.
  11. Preheat an oven to 375 °F, bake for 14-18 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. Tap on the center of the roll, if it sounds hollow then it's done.
  12. Let it cool, serve topped with frosting and enjoy!
