Banana Bread

My mother's banana bread has been one of my favorite treats since I was a kid. This recipe is perfect for when you have a bunch of bananas that are ripening too fast!

Ingredients

  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed bananas (about 4 large or 4 ½ medium ripe bananas)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 7 Tbsp white sugar
  • ½ Tbsp dark brown sugar
  • ½ cup light olive oil
  • ½ + ⅛ cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup flaxseed meal
  • 3 Tbsp hemp seeds
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • ½ cup walnuts, chopped

Steps

Note: instead of 2 pans, this can be baked in a muffin pan. Fill each muffin cup liner to ¾ full, bake for 18-25 minutes.
  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F. Line two 8x4 inch pans with parchment paper.
  2. Mash bananas and measure.
  3. Add the ingredients in order, stir in a large bowl. Fold in the walnuts.
  4. Pour evenly into the two pans and bake for 45-55 minutes.
  5. Cool on a rack for 5 minutes, lift the parchment paper and transfer the loaves to the rack to cool more. Slice and serve.
